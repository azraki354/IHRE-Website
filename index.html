About IHRE

Our Vision

IHRE is an independent, non-governmental, and non-profit organization established to serve and promote humanity in Iceland and globally. We aspire to contribute to social cohesion, promote coexistence and diversity, and enhance the safety of communities for all gender identities, ages, and ethnic or other origins.

Our Guiding Principles

Humanity Neutrality & Independency Do-no-harm Accountability Transparency Integrity

Our Mission & Programs

Gender Identity & Related Issues

We are committed to promoting diversity in gender identities and addressing the challenges faced by groups like the LGBTQIA+ community, who are often marginalized due to traditional gender norms.

Promoting Human Rights through Education

Our work addresses the challenges faced by marginalized groups through evidence-based research, data collection, and direct engagement to create communities free from violence and discrimination.

Social Cohesion & Diversity

We aim to strengthen the social integration of refugees and migrants, enabling them to use and exchange their knowledge and skills as active community members for the overall progress of society.
